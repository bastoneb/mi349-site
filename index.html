Opening Night

Ben Bastone
Oct 25, 2023

The Detroit Pistons opened up the season with a heartbreaking loss to the Miami Heat, 103-102. Cade Cunningham played his first game after missing the whole 2022-2023 season with season ending surgery. He led the team in scoring with 30 points and 9 assists to go with that.
